Choosing Your First Bike

If you're new to motorcycles, one of the first things you'll notice is that not all bikes are created equal. In fact, Most motorcycles have evolved into several distinct categories, each designed around a specific style of riding. Some motorcycles are built to rip across dirt trails, others are meant for high-speed track riding, and some are designed to comfortably cross entire continents. Choosing the right type of motorcycle is one of the most important decisions a rider will make. The design of the bike influences how it handles, how comfortable it feels, where it can be ridden, and what kind of riding experience it provides. On this page, we will discuss several of the most common categories of motorcycles, along with what they're designed to do and why their riders love them.
